How the site meets FBLA criteria
Prepared for Exploring Website Design
  • Home page with navigation — present.
  • Exhibitions page — calendar/list of exhibits with dates & descriptions.
  • Featured contributors (artists/artifacts) — product-card-like layout with image, name, short bio.
  • Visitor information — hours, admission, tickets details included.
  • Responsive design and multi-device viewability. Smooth transitions and scroll reveals to enhance UX.
  • Sources & credits placeholder included in the footer (images and text must be cited if used).
